Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, connect financial accounts, or contact us for support. This includes:

  • Name, email address and password
  • Financial account data (read-only, via secure APIs)
  • Transaction history and balance information
  • Device and usage information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain and improve our Services
  • Aggregate your financial accounts into one unified dashboard
  • Send you technical notices, security alerts and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends

3. Data Security

We employ bank-level 256-bit AES encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest. We have read-only access to your linked accounts — we can never initiate transactions or move your money. We conduct regular security audits and penetration tests.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to third parties. We may share aggregated, anonymized data that does not personally identify you with our partners for analytics purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your data at any time
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@moneytracker.com
